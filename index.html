* COUNTDOWN TO TP INFOTECH DAY 2025 *

DAYS HOURS MINUTES SECONDS

23 January (Thurs) | 3 - 9pm | TP Main Foyer & Auditorium 1 & 2

The Temasek Polytechnic (TP) InfoTech Day is an annual event organised by the School of Informatics & IT. It comprises seminars and a showcase of student projects. The displays at the event feature innovative technology projects completed by final year students. The TP InfoTech Day 2025 microsite offers an online platform for visitors to experience the innovations developed by our students. This microsite enables visitors to view the seminar details and projects online. All projects have been undertaken in collaboration with industry partners and various government agencies, employing the latest technologies in critical areas such as Generative AI & Analytics, Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics, and Intelligent & Immersive Applications.
