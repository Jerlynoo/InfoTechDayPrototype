Key Benefits

  • Improve public cleanliness
  • Prevent pest breeding

Key Technologies Used

  • Unity Game Engine
  • IbisPaint X

Project Background & Description

Community cat feeding has been causing issues such as littering in areas like common areas in HDB estates. Some feeders do not clean up leftover food and water after feeding, which may lead to pests such as rats being attracted to the food. Some feeders also do not use proper containers to contain the food and water.

We developed the Cat Feeder Simulator to teach players the proper ways of feeding community cats, cleaning up after the cats, and managing their feeding areas. In this game, they will play through a variety of levels, all designed to teach specific points about community cat feeding, such as proper tools for cleaning up, and the consequences of leaving food and water out.
