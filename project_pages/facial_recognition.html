Project Background & Description

This project focuses on improving customer service and operational efficiency in the Sugar Loaf Café, part of the Hospitality and Tourism Management (HTM) program at Temasek Polytechnic. To address the challenge of providing personalized service, it integrates Google Glass with advanced facial recognition and AI-driven tools. The system allows waitstaff to identify patrons in real-time and access their drink preferences and dietary restrictions via a hands-free interface. Additionally, a web-based admin app enables seamless management of customer profiles, ensuring the database is up to date with minimal effort.

The initiative not only enhances the café experience but also serves as a training ground for HTM students to engage with emerging technology in hospitality. By incorporating features like speech recognition, data visualization, and multilingual support, the project bridges the gap between technology and customer service. It offers practical exposure to innovations shaping the industry, ensuring students are prepared for tech-driven hospitality environments while delivering superior service quality to patrons.
