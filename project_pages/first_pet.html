Project Background & Description

Some pet owners in Singapore may be unaware of their responsibilities towards their pets, especially in shared spaces. For example, some owners do not leash their pets when they are out and about, which can cause disturbance and inconvenience to others. ​

With this in mind, we partnered with the Municipal Services Office to create First Pet, a top-down RPG that aims to educate its players on the proper ways to care for pets, with an emphasis on municipal issues in Singapore. Players can learn about good practices of pet care through First Pet’s immersive story and by completing various tasks in the game. ​
