Project Background & Description

In today's fast-paced legal environment, lawyers face the critical challenge of efficiently managing an overwhelming volume of documents and information associated with each case. Traditional paper-based systems are no longer sustainable, leading to increased risks of errors, lost files, and inefficiencies that hinder effective case management.

JurisAI is an GenAI-driven platform tailored to the aids of lawyer’s to optimize the workflow by offering a flexible system that manages legal cases. It allows the creation and organization of multiple workspaces for different cases, allowing users to upload, delete and manage documents efficiently. Lawyers can also generate detailed summaries and affidavits directly on the platform. With its’ intelligent chatbot, Jurisai provides informative insights such as evidence analysis, feedback on cases and much more. JurisAI is a tool that simplifies the workflow of a lawyer when it comes to handling cases, ultimately, saving time, improve efficiency and enhance case preparation.
