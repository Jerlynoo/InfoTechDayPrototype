Project Background & Description

​ The Tampines Residents' Network (RN) has been selected to participate in the 2023 Love Our Hood Initiative (LOHI) – Neighborhood Noise Series, organized by the Municipal Services Office (MSO). This initiative focuses on addressing noise-related concerns in local communities, aiming to enhance the quality of life for residents.​ ​

Our project is specifically targeted at Tampines Play@West, a popular park located within the Tampines Terrace precinct, directly opposite Temasek Polytechnic. The goal is to develop innovative solutions that mitigate noise disturbances in this area, ensuring a more peaceful and pleasant environment for the neighborhood.
