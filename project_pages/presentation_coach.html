Project Background & Description

Presentation Coach is an AI Tool designed for students to use to train their oral presentation skills to ensure they have ample preparation and training to produce a captivating and thought-provoking presentation. As students, the developers of this application took note of the weaknesses of their peers in oral presentation due to low confidence, anxiety, and lack of accessible ways of practice.

The application analyses any video uploaded, summarizing it and providing feedback on its contents and any further improvements that can be made such as better eye contact, greater usage in visual aids, and a better manner of speaking.
