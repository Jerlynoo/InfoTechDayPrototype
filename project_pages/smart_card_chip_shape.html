Project Background & Description

Credit cards today feature standard rectangular IC chips. However, this uniformity limits the potential for aesthetic innovation, which is increasingly valued by consumers seeking unique and personalized designs. Currently, designing such cards is a time-intensive and manual process carried out by human graphics designers.

This project addresses these challenges by leveraging Generative AI to redesign the shape of chip based on text description. By generating multiple design variations quickly and accommodating iterative changes efficiently, the project empowers banks and financial institutions to meet customer demand for personalized cards while minimizing designer workload and speeding up the overall design process.
