Project Background & Description

When considering the allocation of resources to enhance residents' career and employment knowledge in specific fields or industries, one of the most reliable data sources are government agencies focusing on human resource, career and jobs.

However, oftentimes, data from such agencies may be incomplete or unstructured. Community organizations and local programs struggle to align their efforts with residents' career interests, leading to inefficient use of time, money and effort.

A comprehensive data pipeline is proposed which begins with data cleaning, progressing to automated data visualization, and culminating in an advanced machine learning (ML) model to enhance the job search process for the residents of North-East Singapore.
